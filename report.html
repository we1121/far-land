Reporting Guidelines

Be Specific

Include exact locations, dates, and detailed descriptions to help us verify and update information accurately.

Be Objective

Focus on factual information rather than subjective opinions. This helps other travelers make informed decisions.

Include Visual Evidence

When possible, include photos to support your report. This is especially helpful for infrastructure changes.

Respect Privacy

Do not include personal information about other travelers or locals in your reports or images.
